The Hidden Cost of Waiting#

Every day you wonder but don’t ask, you’re potentially overpaying. The average company wastes 32% on AWS - often because no one wants to bother engineering with ‘just a question.’

Common Questions That Go Unasked#

  1. Graviton Migration: AWS Graviton instances offer 20-40% better price-performance. Ask ‘Which instances can migrate to Graviton?’ Get specific answers with savings amounts.

  2. Unattached EBS Volumes: You have snapshots. Why pay for both? Ask your Cloud Expert: ‘Do I have unattached volumes?’ Get instant clarity.

  3. Elastic IP Waste: Unused Elastic IPs cost money. Stop wondering. Start asking. Get answers.

  4. CloudWatch Log Retention: Logs kept forever consume storage. Ask ‘What’s my CloudWatch bill?’ Know immediately, don’t guess.

  5. Idle Instance Detection: EC2 instances running under 5% CPU for weeks are costing you hundreds monthly. Ask your bot: ‘Do I have idle instances?’ Get the answer instantly.
